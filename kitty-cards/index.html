Cat cards is a turn-based card game where players fills the board.

The player with highest points wins.

You start with two cards. A card is between 1 to 6.

On your turn, you can either play a card or draw a card.

To play a card, click on the card and click a cell to move to.

  • Card + same color cell = 2x value of card.
  • Card + white cell = value of card.
  • Card + wrong color cell = 0

Good luck!
