This is a turn-based game: each player fills the board with cards. The cup's color determines the awarded points.

To win, you need to have the highest points on the board.

You start the game with two cards.

On your turn, you can either play a card or draw a card from the draw deck.

To play a card, choose the card to place on the board and select a cell to move the card to.

  • Placing a card on a cup of the same color = 2x value of that card.
  • Placing a colored card on a blank teacup = 1x value on the card.
  • Placing a colored card on a colored cup of the wrong color = 0 points

Good luck!
